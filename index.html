Policy Relaxation Recommendations

All recommendations are within pre-approved ranges per Section 7 of the Credit Policy. Combined Tier 1 impact: +739 incremental approvals, moving approval rate from 58.9% to ~59.9%.

S1: Raise DTI Cap for RS3–5 from 70% to 75% TIER 1 — RECOMMENDED

+348 incremental approvals • Avg PD: 10.95% • Already priced at 21–32% APR
RS3: +171 apps (PD 6.9%, Vantage 663) • RS4: +117 (PD 13.0%, Vantage 642) • RS5: +60 (PD 18.6%, Vantage 631)
Pre-approved range: 60%–80% max DTI. Moderate risk applicants just 1–5pp above threshold.

S4: Raise Utilization Cap from >100% to >110% (CCE8) TIER 1 — RECOMMENDED

+391 incremental approvals • Avg PD: 10.26%
Sole-reason declines where slight over-utilization is the only failing rule.
Pre-approved range: 90%–110%. Within policy notification procedure.

S3: Relax Charge-off Recency from ≤12mo to ≤6mo (CCE6) TIER 2 — MODERATE

+374 incremental approvals • Avg PD: 13.54% • Avg Vantage: 567
Charge-offs 7–12 months ago are less predictive of near-term default than recent ones.
Pre-approved range: 0–24 months. Recommend pairing with tighter monitoring.

+168 incremental approvals • Avg PD: 25.18%
High-risk RS6 applicants priced at 33% APR. Pricing covers risk but loss rates could be meaningful.
Higher risk appetite required.
